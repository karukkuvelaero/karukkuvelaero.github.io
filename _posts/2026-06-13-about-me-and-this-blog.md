# Welcome

Hello and welcome!

I'm Karukkuvel Raj, an Avionics Engineer currently working in civil business jet systems engineering. My professional experience includes aircraft systems development, landing gear control units, power distribution systems, and engine electrical interfaces.

This website serves as both my professional portfolio and personal knowledge hub. Here, I document my engineering journey, share technical insights, explore research interests, review books and movies, and write about topics that inspire me.

Whether you're a fellow engineer, recruiter, researcher, student, or simply a curious reader, I hope you find something valuable here.
