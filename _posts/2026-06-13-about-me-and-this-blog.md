## Welcome

Hello and welcome!

I'm **Karukkuvel Raj D**, an Avionics Systems Engineer currently working in civil business jet systems engineering. My professional experience spans aircraft systems development, landing gear control units, power distribution systems, and engine electrical interfaces. I hold an **M.E. in Avionics** from Madras Institute of Technology, Chennai, and the **INCOSE ASEP** certification in Systems Engineering.

This website serves as both my professional portfolio and personal knowledge hub. Here I document my engineering journey, share technical insights, explore research interests, and write about topics that interest me beyond work — books, movies, and ideas worth thinking about.

Whether you are a fellow engineer, recruiter, researcher, student, or simply a curious reader, I hope you find something valuable here.
