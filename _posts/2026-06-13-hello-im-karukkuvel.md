## What Do I Actually Do?

Aerospace is a broad field, so let me be specific.

### Currently
I work at **Vijigi Intellect Private Limited**, Pune, on **civil business jet avionics systems**. My day-to-day involves:

- Defining how aircraft systems talk to each other (interfaces between landing gear, engines, and power systems)
- Writing and reviewing technical requirements for avionics subsystems
- Verifying that systems meet those requirements — following the **ARP4754A** standard (the aerospace industry's process guideline for developing aircraft systems)

### Previously
At **Killis Bird LLP**, I worked as a **Flight Controls Engineer** on UAV (unmanned aerial vehicle) systems. I integrated open-source autopilot software (PX4) onto multiple drone platforms — VTOL hybrids, fixed-wing aircraft, and quadcopters — and conducted actual flight tests.

I also built system architecture models using a tool called **Capella**, which is the aerospace industry's standard for Model-Based Systems Engineering (MBSE).
